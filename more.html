More information on Deceptium

Description: Deceptium is a cient capable of bypassing Lockdown Browser's test locking feature by utilizing a major flaw in their functionality, this includes Lockdown Browser integrated apps like Performance Matters.

Discovery: This exploit was discovered by X-88 somewhere around October to November of 2023, when he was testing iframe generating bookmarklets in his Performance Matters test, finding out it didn't lock him out even when he interacted with the web content inside the Iframe

Updates: Deceptium's newest variant is Deceptium V2 (Bookmarklet) which adds more features and design than Deceptium V1 along with being reduced to 1 bookmarklet. V2 has newly added features including a search bar (for URLs and Bing), an app window from CybriaOS design and cloaking/hiding features to prevent teachers from seeing Deceptium on your screen

Planned Updates: Our plan is to branch Deceptium into 3 forms, Bookmarklet, Chrome Extension and Terminal, as the bookmarklet is only limited to ChromeOS or Chromium based users, we hope to spread Deceptium into other Operating Systems so that You can ace your tests and get the grades you need
